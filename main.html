The Pyramids of Giza

The Pyramids of Giza are ancient monumental tombs in Egypt, built over 4,500 years ago for Pharaohs Khufu, Khafre, and Menkaure, and are one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World.

Cairo- The Capital

Cairo is the capital of Egypt, known for its rich history, bustling markets, and proximity to iconic landmarks like the Pyramids of Giza and the Sphinx.
